# BootstrapMade — Overview

## What BootstrapMade Represents

BootstrapMade is a professional template marketplace producing HTML/CSS/JS website templates built on Bootstrap and progressively adopting CSS Grid and modern layout techniques. It is the internet's dominant source of "business-grade" template patterns — used by hundreds of thousands of small businesses, startups, non-profits, agencies, restaurants, and corporate sites worldwide.

Within Holocraft's reference hierarchy, BootstrapMade occupies a specific and deliberate position: **the floor, not the ceiling**. It represents the minimum acceptable quality bar for a professional website — structured, conversion-optimized, readable, and trustworthy. Holocraft's output should exceed it in every visual dimension while extracting and amplifying its structural intelligence.

Do not dismiss it as "generic." Generic is not accidental — it is engineered. The patterns here are distilled from conversion data, user behavior research, and what actually works for real businesses selling real things to real people.

## Core Philosophy

**Clarity over cleverness.** Every design decision prioritizes comprehension. A user landing on a BootstrapMade site immediately understands: what the business does, what the next action is, and why they should trust this organization.

**Structure as content.** The grid is not a container — it is a communication tool. Content arranged in a 3-column feature grid communicates "three equal pillars." A single-column CTA section commands full attention. BootstrapMade sites are architecturally legible at a glance.

**Conversion optimization as a first principle.** CTA buttons appear in the hero, mid-page, and footer. Trust signals (testimonials, client logos, stats) are placed at predictable positions. Forms are minimal. Friction is reduced at every step.

**Broad accessibility by default.** High color contrast, readable font sizes, semantic HTML. These templates serve everyone from a local dentist's office to a VC-backed startup demo. Accessibility is structural, not cosmetic.

## What Holocraft Extracts from BootstrapMade

### Section Structure Patterns
The proven landing page sequence: Hero → Features/Services → About → Stats/Social Proof → Testimonials → CTA → Pricing → FAQ → Contact → Footer. This order is not arbitrary — it mirrors the psychological journey from awareness to trust to conversion. Holocraft uses this sequence as the default narrative arc and deviates deliberately when the client's context requires it.

### Navigation Patterns
Sticky header behavior, transparent-to-solid on scroll, hamburger collapse breakpoints at 768px, dropdown menu behavior with keyboard accessibility, active state scroll-spy. These are solved problems. Extract the pattern and rebuild the visual.

### CTA Placement Science
- **Primary CTA**: in hero, above the fold, paired with a secondary "learn more" link
- **Soft CTA**: mid-page after testimonials or features — once trust is established, invite action
- **Hard CTA section**: full-width contrasting background section, 1-2 lines of copy + primary button, placed before footer
- **Footer CTAs**: secondary contact links, newsletter signup

### Form Design Principles
Minimal fields (remove every field that isn't essential for the first conversion step), inline validation messages, unambiguous submit button copy ("Get Started Free" beats "Submit"), clear success/error states. Forms are never creative — they are maximally functional.

### Card Component Information Hierarchy
Icon/image at top → heading (2-4 words) → body text (2-3 sentences max) → optional CTA link. This hierarchy is the most reused UI pattern across the web. Extract it, elevate the visual execution.

### Section Alternation Rhythm
White background → light grey (#f8f9fa) background → white → colored accent background. This rhythm creates visual flow without complex animations. It is a spatial pacing technique used universally because it works.

## What NOT to Copy

**Do not copy the visual style.** BootstrapMade aesthetics are intentionally generic — they serve the widest possible audience by offending the fewest. For Holocraft clients, generic is a failure mode.

Specifically do not replicate:
- Thin Bootstrap-blue (#3498db or #0d6efd) as a primary palette with no personality
- Stock photography composed identically to every other template (person at laptop, handshake, team meeting)
- Flat box-shadow cards with no character (`box-shadow: 0 2px 15px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`)
- The section heading pattern: centered h2 + 2px colored underline + grey subtitle — visually exhausted
- Font combinations that are readable but forgettable (Open Sans body + Raleway heading)

**The extraction principle:** Take the skeleton (section sequence, card information hierarchy, CTA placement timing, form field reduction), discard the skin (color palette, typefaces, stock images, generic icons), and rebuild the skin with Holocraft's premium visual system — dark mode, editorial typography, purposeful motion, and brand-specific color.
